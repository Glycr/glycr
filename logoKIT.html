Usage Guidelines
Keep the brand consistent everywhere
Use on approved backgroundsDark slate, white, and brand teal surfaces work. Always check contrast ratio > 4.5:1 for accessibility.
Don't stretch or distortAlways maintain the original aspect ratio. Never squash, skew, rotate, or recolour the mark.
Maintain clear spaceKeep a minimum clear space of 1× the icon height around all sides of the logo on every surface.
Don't change the colorsUse only approved brand colors. Never apply gradients, drop shadows, or off-brand tints to the logo.
Use the right variantHorizontal for navbars. Icon mark only when "Glycr" text is already visible nearby. Teal variant on green surfaces.
Don't place on busy backgroundsAvoid the logo over complex photos or patterns without a semi-transparent dark overlay beneath it.
